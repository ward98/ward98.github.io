Biography

 Upon completing at BA Geography degree at The University of Liverpool I have taken a keen interest in spatial data science, combined with pressing issue of climate change. This has facilitated me to join up with the Geographic Data Science Lab to pursue a PhD with Data Analytics and Society as part of the CDT. This is looking at energy efficiency of the UK's housing stock with a prediction if the country can achieve its 2050 carbon targets.
